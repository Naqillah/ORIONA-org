About ORIONA


ORIONA is a refugee-led community organization committed to fostering psycho-social and mental health support, education, creative expressions, economic inclusion and community engagement. It serves as a bridge between refugee communities, host communities, organizations and stakeholders. We are committed to fostering collaboration, growth and empowerment.
With our operations based in refugee settings, we dedicate ourselves to the values of equity, inclusivity, professionalism, transparency and social impact.

Our Programs


Peer Counseling Clubs

Safe and inclusive spaces for youth to openly share challenges and receive guided peer support.

Mental Health Education

Life skills development, resilience building and mental wellness awareness programs.

Creative Expression

Using art, storytelling and innovation as tools for emotional healing and empowerment.
