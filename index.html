Empowering Refugee Youth Through Mental Health & Community

ORIONA is a refugee-led community organization fostering psycho-social support, education, creative expression and youth empowerment in Kakuma Refugee Camp.

About ORIONA


ORIONA is a refugee-led community organization committed to fostering psycho-social and mental health support, education, creative expressions, economic inclusion and community engagement. It serves as a bridge between refugee communities, host communities, organizations and stakeholders. We are committed to fostering collaboration, growth and empowerment.
With our operations based in refugee settings, we dedicate ourselves to the values of equity, inclusivity, professionalism, transparency and social impact.

Mission Statement

To empower youth, particularly refugees, by providing opportunities in education, psycho-social and mental health support, creative expressions, social and economic programmes, while fostering collaboration between refugee communities, host communities, organizations and stakeholders.

Goals

  • To establish a strong networking and social hub for refugee and host community youth.
  • To bridge the gap between refugee communities, local organizations, businesses, security personnel, NGOs, UN agencies and other relevant stakeholders.
  • To support youth in accessing educational, economic, psycho-social and mental health support, social and economic opportunities.
  • To promote sustainable development through community engagement programs.

Objectives

  • Facilitate mentorship, skills training and educational programs.
  • Advocate for the rights and welfare of refugee youth.
  • Organize community engagement programs and social empowerment projects.
  • Develop partnerships to promote inclusion, diversity and sustainable development.
  • Provide safe spaces for refugee youth empowerment and development.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Commitment to ethical and honest operations.
  • Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and promoting equal opportunities.
  • Accountability: Ensuring transparency in governance and financial management.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity in addressing youth development challenges.
  • Collaboration: Strengthening partnerships for sustainable growth.

Membership

Membership is open to registered refugees and allies from host communities who support ORIONA's mission and objectives.

Membership applications must be submitted through the organization's official website, registrar's office, or email.
